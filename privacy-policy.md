# Privacy Policy for HIIT Timer

*Last updated: May 2, 2026*

HIIT Timer is a free fitness timer app. This privacy policy explains how we handle information.

## Data Collection

HIIT Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your custom workouts and settings locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because no data is collected.
